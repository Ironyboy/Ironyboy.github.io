Introduction

Welcome to our Animals Webpage! Learn about the fascinating world of mammals, birds, and reptiles.

Mammals

Mammals are warm-blooded vertebrates characterized by the presence of mammary glands, which enable them to nurse their young. Examples include lions, elephants, and dolphins.

Birds

Birds are feathered, winged, two-legged, warm-blooded creatures. They exhibit a wide range of behaviors and adaptations. Examples include eagles, penguins, and parrots.

Reptiles

Reptiles are cold-blooded vertebrates that include snakes, turtles, and crocodiles. They are known for their scaly skin and laying eggs. Learn more about their diverse characteristics.
